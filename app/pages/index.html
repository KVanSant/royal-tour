A generation revisited

What’s old is new again. At least that’s how we feel at Royal Tour. Handcrafting the right balance between that classic look and a modern feel is at the heart of each individual ride. The Golden Age of handbuilt bikes is here again, and Royal Tour is at the forefront of artistry, function, and of course elegance.

Our wheels are always turning

Royal Tour, based in Atlanta, sprang from a simple idea. Innovate, learn and never play it safe. With that mantra as our backbone, doing what we love perpetuates a long running heritage of comfort, design and elegance all handcrafted for you, our rider. Satisfaction guaranteed.
