The Edward

Named after famed English royalty "Edward The Black Prince of Whales". True to it's name sake, this American roadster painted in ink high gloss black is countered with light wood and leather components. The American (tan) Cherry wood grip,fenders, and downtube shifter along with cream colored wheels and camel tan colored leather saddle give it the perfect black & tan contrast.

Starting at $2,499.00
